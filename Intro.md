# Personal Profile

I am a Computer Engineering student currently in my 4th semester at the Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology (GIKI).

This repository documents my work with **Maktab-e-Digital (MeD)**, where I am actively engaged in learning and implementing concepts related to RISC-V architecture, processor design, and digital systems.

# About Maktab-e-Digital (MeD)

Maktab-e-Digital (MeD) is a learning and research-driven initiative focused on developing practical skills in emerging technologies. It provides a platform for students to gain hands-on experience in fields such as computer architecture, embedded systems, digital design, and AI-oriented hardware.

Through structured programs and project-based learning, MeD emphasizes building strong foundational knowledge alongside real-world implementation skills.

# Technical Interests

My primary areas of interest lie at the intersection of hardware design and system-level efficiency:

* Digital Design & RTL: Designing datapaths, control units, and hardware modules using HDL
* Hardware Acceleration: Exploring optimized hardware solutions for performance-critical tasks
* AI-Oriented Hardware: Studying architectural support for machine learning workloads
